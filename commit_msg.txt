🧹 Optimize `GetPhotos` Initial Load Performance

🎯 What
Deferred S3 presigned URL generation during `GetPhotos` by implementing an authenticated proxy endpoint (`/api/images/s3/{photoId}`).

💡 Why
`GetPhotos` loads the entire gallery and synchronously loops over every photo to generate presigned AWS S3 URLs. Even when generated concurrently via `Task.WhenAll`, this CPU-bound operations scales poorly to hundreds/thousands of photos. By deferring the generation to a proxy endpoint mapped to the `ImagesController`, the `GetPhotos` response payload handles almost instantaneously and S3 URLs are only generated for photos that are actually lazy-loaded by the browser. The frontend interceptor handles appending the JWT.

✅ Verification
- Ensured backend builds and `PhotosController` tests pass.
- Ensured the `ImagesController` incorporates the same group-access DB verification.
- Verified frontend checks.

✨ Result
Gallery load times are drastically decreased without breaking client-side filtering.
